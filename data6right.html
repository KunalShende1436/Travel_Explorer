Varanasi

Geographic Loaction:

Varanasi, also known as Benares or Kashi, is located in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Situated on the banks of the Ganges River, it is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.



Tadoba National Park Attractions:

  1. Dashashwamedh Ghat:

    Witness the Ganga Aarti, a mesmerizing ritual involving fire, chanting, and rhythmic drumming, which takes place every evening at Dashashwamedh Ghat.

  2. Kashi Vishwanath Temple:

    The Kashi Vishwanath Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in Varanasi, India. It is one of the holiest temples and a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus. The temple has undergone several constructions and renovations throughout history and holds immense cultural and religious importance.

  3. Ghats and Ganges River:

    The city is famous for its numerous ghats (steps leading to the river) along the Ganges, each with its unique significance. The Ganges River is believed to have the power to wash away sins.

  4. Temples and Shrines:

    Explore the numerous temples, including the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple, and the Durga Temple, each reflecting the city's rich religious and cultural heritage.
