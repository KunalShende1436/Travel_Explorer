Ayodhya

Geographic Loaction:

Ayodhya is situated in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India, on the banks of the Sarayu River. It holds immense religious significance in Hinduism as the birthplace of Lord Rama.



Ayodhya Attractions:

  1. Ram Janmabhoomi:

    Visit the Ram Janmabhoomi, believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama. The site has the Ram Janmabhoomi Temple and attracts pilgrims from across the world.

  2. Religious Significance:

    Ayodhya is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, a central figure in the Hindu epic Ramayana. The city is dotted with temples, ghats, and sacred sites associated with Lord Rama's life.

  3. Hanuman Garhi:

    Explore the Hanuman Garhi, a temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, situated on a hill. It provides panoramic views of the city.

  4. Sita Ki Rasoi:

    Discover Sita Ki Rasoi, a temple associated with Sita, showcasing an ancient kitchen where it is believed she used to cook
