Taj Mahal,Agra

Geographic Loaction:

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It sits on the southern bank of the Yamuna River and is easily accessible from major cities like Delhi.



Taj Mahal Attractions:

  1. Architectural Masterpiece:

    The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of enduring love, known for its exquisite Mughal architecture, intricate marble inlays, and stunning symmetry.

  2. Symbol of Love:

    Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal is a testament to the eternal love between the emperor and his queen.

  3. Taj Mahal:

    The main attraction, the Taj Mahal, is a white marble mausoleum with a central dome and four minarets. It is especially breathtaking during sunrise and sunset.

  4. Agra Fort:

    Explore the Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its red sandstone architecture and historical significance. The fort offers panoramic views of the Taj Mahal.
