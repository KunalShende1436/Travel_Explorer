Mysore

Geographic Loaction:

Mysuru (Mysore) is a city located in the southern part of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is approximately 140 kilometers southwest of Bengaluru.



Mysore Attractions:

  1. Royal Heritage:

    Mysuru is renowned for its royal history, with Mysuru Palace being a prominent symbol of the city's majestic past. The intricate architecture and opulent interiors showcase the grandeur of the Wodeyar dynasty.

  2. Chamundi Hills:

    Climb to the top of Chamundi Hills to visit the Chamundeshwari Temple and enjoy panoramic views of Mysuru.

  3. Dasara Festival:

    Experience the grand Dasara festival, a 10-day celebration held annually, featuring a colorful procession, cultural events, and a torchlight parade. The city comes alive with vibrant decorations during this time.

  4. Devaraja Market:

    Experience the local culture at Devaraja Market, known for its vibrant atmosphere and a wide array of spices, silk, sandalwood products, and traditional Mysore paintings.
