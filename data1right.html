Lakshadweep Island India

Geographic Loaction:

Lakshadweep is a group of islands in the Arabian Sea, located off the southwestern coast of India. It's known for its coral atolls and beautiful lagoons.



Lakshadweep Island Attractions:

  1. Agatti Island:

    Known for its beautiful beaches and coral reefs, Agatti is a great spot for water activities like snorkeling and scuba diving.

  2. Bangaram Island:

    A picturesque island with pristine beaches, coral reefs, and turquoise lagoons, perfect for relaxation and water sports.

  3. Kavaratti Island:

    The capital of Lakshadweep, Kavaratti is known for its mosques, the Marine Aquarium, and water sports facilities.

  4. Kalpeni Island:

    Famous for its three uninhabited islets called Tilakkam, Pitti, and Cheriyam, offering a serene and unspoiled environment.
