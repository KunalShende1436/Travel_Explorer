Bombay

Geographic Loaction:

Mumbai is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra, situated on the west coast of India. It is the financial, commercial, and entertainment hub of the country.



Bombay Attractions:

  1. Bollywood:

    Mumbai is the heart of the Indian film industry, Bollywood. Consider taking a studio tour or catching a film screening to get a glimpse of the glamorous world of Indian cinema.

  2. Marine Drive:

    Take a leisurely walk along Marine Drive, also known as the Queen's Necklace, to enjoy the scenic views, especially during sunset.

  3. Chowpatty Beach:

    Experience the lively ambiance at Chowpatty Beach, known for its festivals, beach games, and delectable street food.

  4. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT):

    Explore this UNESCO World Heritage Site, an architectural masterpiece that serves as a major railway station and reflects Mumbai's colonial history.
