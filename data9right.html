Kedarnath

Geographic Loaction:

Kedarnath is a town located in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand, India. It is nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas, surrounded by majestic peaks and lies along the Mandakini River.



Kedarnath Attractions:

  1. Kedarnath Temple:

    Visit the ancient Kedarnath Temple, built in stone and located at an elevation of about 3,583 meters. It is a key pilgrimage site and is believed to have been constructed by Adi Shankaracharya.

  2. Chorabari Tal (Gandhi Sarovar):

    Explore Chorabari Tal, also known as Gandhi Sarovar, a pristine lake located at the base of the Chorabari Bamak Glacier.

  3. Vasuki Tal:

    Trek to Vasuki Tal, a high-altitude glacial lake surrounded by snow-capped peaks, providing a serene and picturesque setting.

  4. Bhairavnath Temple:

    Pay a visit to the Bhairavnath Temple, located near the Kedarnath Temple. Bhairavnath is considered the guardian deity of Kedarnath during the winter months.
