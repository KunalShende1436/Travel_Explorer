Tadoba National Park

Geographic Loaction:

Tadoba National Park is located in the Chandrapur district of Maharashtra, India. It's situated in the central part of the state and is part of the Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve.



Tadoba National Park Attractions:

  1. Bengal Tigers:

    Tadoba is renowned for its significant population of Bengal tigers. Safari trips offer a chance to spot these majestic creatures in their natural habitat.

  2. Rich Biodiversity:

    Apart from tigers, the park is home to diverse flora and fauna, including leopards, sloth bears, Indian bison (gaur), and various species of deer.

  3. Tiger Photography:

    Wildlife photographers find Tadoba a rewarding destination, capturing stunning images of tigers and other wildlife in their natural surroundings.

  4. Tiger Reserve Safari:

    Take a safari through the reserve to spot tigers and other wildlife species. Both morning and evening safaris are available.
