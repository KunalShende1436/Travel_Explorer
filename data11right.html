Ujjain

Geographic Loaction:



Ujjain Attractions:

  1. Mahakaleshwar Temple:

    Visit the Mahakaleshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and witness the Bhasma Aarti, a sacred ritual involving the application of holy ash.

  2. Kaal Bhairav Temple:

    Explore the Kaal Bhairav Temple, dedicated to Lord Bhairav, a fierce form of Shiva. It is believed to be one of the eight Bhairavas of Ujjain.

  3. Harsiddhi Temple:

    Visit the Harsiddhi Temple, dedicated to Goddess Annapurna, for its architectural beauty and religious significance.

  4. Kumbh Mela:

    Ujjain hosts the Simhastha Kumbh Mela, one of the largest religious gatherings, attracting millions of pilgrims and tourists. The Kumbh Mela occurs once every 12 years and is a unique cultural and spiritual experience.
