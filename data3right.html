Diksha Bhumi

Geographic Loaction:

Diksha Bhumi is located in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. It's a sacred Buddhist site and a significant pilgrimage destination for followers of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar.



Tadoba National Park Attractions:

  1. Architectural Significance:

    The site features a grand stupa known as Diksha Bhumi Stupa. The architecture reflects Buddhist design principles and serves as a symbol of the Buddhist revival in India.

  2. Buddhist Pilgrimage Site:

    Diksha Bhumi holds immense importance in Buddhism as it is the place where Dr. B. R. Ambedkar, a key figure in the Indian Buddhist movement, converted to Buddhism along with a large number of followers

  3. Diksha Bhumi Stupa:

    The main attraction, the stupa, is a monumental structure symbolizing the Buddhist teachings. It is often illuminated beautifully during special events.

  4. Dr. Ambedkar Memorial:

    Explore the Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Memorial to learn about his life, contributions, and the significance of his conversion to Buddhism.
